Our Process

Here's what happens after you send us your task.
* We'll review your task and assign it to an Excel Rescue expert developer.
* If we have any questions, we'll let you know straight away. Once we're clear on your task, we'll provide you with a no obligation quote.
* Upon acceptance of our quote, and receipt of your payment, we'll get straight to work.
* You receive the completed task.

Our Commitment to You

Some details about how we'll work together.
* Pricing is in Naira.
* Target turnaround time is 2-3 business days once work begins.
* Money back guarantee on all Small, Medium and Large tasks. The Money Back Guarantee does not apply on Ongoing hourly tasks.
* Strict security and non-disclosure agreement

Remote Assistance

Optionally, we can work on your spreadsheet remotely.
Sometimes our strict security and non-disclosure agreement isn't enough. For example, if you're prohibited from sending your spreadsheet outside your company network. In that case, we can complete the necessary changes remotely. This is done over web conference call and you'll see every mouse click that happens, as it happens (and retain full control).
Please let us know up-front if you need your task completed remotely, so we can include the cost in the quote. This will increase the cost, so please only choose this option if absolutely necessary.
